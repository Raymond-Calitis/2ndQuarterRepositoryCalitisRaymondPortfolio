Arena 11: Electro Valley

Hog Rider
Ice Golem
Musketeer
Ice Spirit
Skeletons
Cannon
Fireball
Log

Why This Deck?

The 2.6 Hog cycle, this deck is famous because it is very fast and cheap. You can play your Hog Rider over and over to constantly pressure your opponent's tower. It uses cheap cards like Skeletons and Ice Spirit to defend powerfully for little cost. This saves your elixir so you can attack again while your opponent is unprepared. Winning requires skill and practice, but it can beat almost any other deck. It's a classic strategy that has been strong for a long time.
